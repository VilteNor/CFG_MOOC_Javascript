If you are an individual

Thank you for showing interest in helping to make the community around you a better place. Since the current cost of living crisis many families up and down the country are struggling to afford to buy food, are skipping meals and there are children going hungry as a result.

This is where you come in.

You can help by participating in the below initaitves or creating your own programs to encourage sustainable food use and help feed those in need during this testing time that will benefit people and the wider community.

Here you can find how you can help those who struggle with the cost of living crisis.

  • If you want to help, please donate food items, money or volunteer at your local food bank. You can find a food bank local to you here:Trussel Trust Food banks   and here:Food aid network

  • Many food banks will provide information on what food items they need and will tell you the location of their food collection points, so check their website or social media to know what they need and where to donate food.

  • Support the Feed the Future campaign and use your voice to get Free School Meals extended to include all children from families on Universal Credit. You can find out more and how to support the campaign: Feed the Future Campaign
