If you are a business owner

Thank you for showing interest in helping to make the community around you and your business a better place. Since the current cost of living crisis many families up and down the country are struggling to afford to buy food, are skipping meals and there are children going hungry as a result.

This is where your business comes in.

Your business, your employees, friends and family can help by participating in the below initaitves or creating your own programs to encourage sustainable food use and help feed those in need during this testing time that will benefit people and the wider community.

Here you can find out how your business can help those who struggle with the cost of living crisis.

  • If you are a business and want to help, throw your weight behind the Feed the Future campaign and amplify the campaign to get Free School Meals extended to include all children from families on Universal Credit. You can find out more and how to support the campaign: Feed the Future Campaign

  • Please run initiatives with your employees, partners and families to donate food items, money or volunteer time with local food banks. You can find a food bank local to your business here:Trussel Trust Food banks   and here:Food aid network

  • Many food banks will provide information on what food items they need and will tell you the location of their food collection points, so check their website or social media to know what they need and where to donate food.
